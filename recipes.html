Kale with Tomato and Onion

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lb fresh kale
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped tomato
  • 3/4 cup chopped tomato
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 to 3/4 tsp pepper

Directions:

  1. Wash kale, do not dry. Tear into small pieces
  2. Place kale into dutch oven, do not add water
  3. Cook uncovered 20-30 minutes or until tender
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet under low heat
  5. Add tomato and onion to oil
  6. Cook on medium heat, stirring constantly until tender
  7. Add cooked greens and other ingredients, stirring constantly for 15 minutes
  8. Serve
